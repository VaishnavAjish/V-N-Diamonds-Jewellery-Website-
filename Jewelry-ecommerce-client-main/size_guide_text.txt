1.Print Instructions

US Letter size (8½ × 11 inches)
 page.

 to ensure accurate measurements.

credit card

3.Finding Your Bracelet Size

Option 1: Using a Measuring Tape

Wrap a soft measuring tape around your wrist just below the wrist bone (where you would normally wear a bracelet).

Note the measurement in 
inches or centimeters
.

Add 
½ inch (1.3 cm)
 for a snug fit or 
1 inch (2.5 cm)
 for a looser fit.

Option 2: Using a String or Strip of Paper

Wrap a 
string
 or 
strip of paper
 around your wrist.

Mark where it overlaps.

Measure the length with a ruler to find your wrist size.
