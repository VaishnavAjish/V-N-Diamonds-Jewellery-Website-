HARENEs Bracelet Size Chart Instructions

1.Print Instructions

  • Print this guide on a full US Letter size (8½ × 11 inches) page.

  • Scale to 100% to ensure accurate measurements.

2.Check Printing Accuracy

Place a credit card on the line below. It should match the size of the printed line.
PLACE CREDIT CARD HERE

3.Finding Your Bracelet Size

Option 1: Using a Measuring Tape

  1. Wrap a soft measuring tape around your wrist just below the wrist bone (where you would normally wear a bracelet).

  2. Note the measurement in inches or centimeters.

  3. Add ½ inch (1.3 cm) for a snug fit or 1 inch (2.5 cm) for a looser fit.

Option 2: Using a String or Strip of Paper

    1. Wrap a string or strip of paper around your wrist.

    2. Mark where it overlaps.

    3. Measure the length with a ruler to find your wrist size.
